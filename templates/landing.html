About DamCerti

DamCerti was built by Damil Sajjad to solve a simple, repetitive time consuming problem: generating certificates one by one is slow, error-prone, and unnecessary. Whether you're running a course, a workshop, a hackathon, or an event, DamCerti turns a spreadsheet of names and a certificate design into a complete, personalized batch in seconds...not hours.

Our Mission

We believe recognizing people's achievements shouldn't require design skills or expensive software. DamCerti exists to make professional-quality certificate generation accessible to educators, event organizers, and communities of any size -- free to start, simple to use, and built to scale with you.
